Ingredients

  • 1 16 oz ball of pizza dough
  • 1 Tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 cup of pizza sauce
  • 3 ounces of thinly sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 5 ounces of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2.5 ounces of pepperoni
  • 2 tablespoons of shredded parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Place a pizza stone in the bottom third of your oven.
  2. Preheat your oven to 550 degrees F
  3. Prepare a work surface.
  4. Roll out the dough
  5. Transfer the dough to parchment paper
  6. Par bake for 1 to 2 minutes
  7. Top your pizza
  8. Bake the pizza
  9. Remove, let cool, and enjoy!
