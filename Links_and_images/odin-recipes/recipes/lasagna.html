Lasagna

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base.
Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly!
This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • Meat Sauce
  • 1/2 pound of dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 pound freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directioms

  1. Boil noodles for 10 minutes
  2. Drain the noodles
  3. Heat oven to 375 degrees
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake
  6. Cool and Serve!
