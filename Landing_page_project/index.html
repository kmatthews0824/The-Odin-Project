The members of Bridge 4

Kaladin is the leader of Bridge 4. As a slave, he stepped up to lead the bridge crew out of their slavery and into the ranks of warriors

Rock, a horneater, is the renown chef of the Bridge 4 crew and is Kaladin's right hand man

Lopen is the optimistic member of Bridge 4. When all else fails, count on this Herdazen to lighten up the mood

Moash, the mysterious one. Disliking Kaladin's leadership at first, he is currently (as of book 2) one of Kaladin's most trusted members.
